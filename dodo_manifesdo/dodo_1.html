Aha Moments!

You know that feeling when you’ve been slamming your head against the wall because you haven’t gotten the hang of a sport or solve that equation? Only after hours, weeks or months of practicing will a person get that aha moment when blank activity finally makes sense! The aha moment is so special because it is completely derived from the act of working hard. dOdO games are centered around these aha moments! Perhaps these will not be easy games but after pushing through, players will experience the bliss of an aha moment!
