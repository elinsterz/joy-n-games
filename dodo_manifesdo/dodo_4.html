BOLDNESS & ABSURDITY

So often people are forced to be polite and quiet because otherwise they'd be considered rude. Like how awful would it be if someone started burping while the teacher was giving a lecture? While the rules of society require people to behave a certain way, dOdO games carves a space to be unapologetically bold and absurd. These games are designed for people to play without reserve! When entering a dOdO game, the person is accepted into a world where weirdness is accepted, appreciated and even applauded.
